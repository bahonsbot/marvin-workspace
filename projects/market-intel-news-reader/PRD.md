# PRD: Market Intel News Reader App

## Status: ✅ COMPLETE (Feb 28, 2026)

## 1. Executive Summary

**Problem:** Philippe wants a personalized news reader on iPhone that filters news based on Market Intel patterns (geopolitical, financial, macro, etc.) instead of generic news.

**Solution:** A simple iOS-compatible web app (PWA) that displays news from our RSS/Reddit monitors, filtered by Market Intel categories.

**Target:** Philippe (personal use)

---

## 2. Concept

### What it does:
- Shows news items from our RSS feeds + Reddit
- Filters by Market Intel pattern categories:
  - 🌍 Geopolitical (wars, sanctions, conflicts)
  - 💰 Financial (banks, credit, crises)
  - 📈 Macro (Fed, inflation, GDP)
  - 🏢 Corporate (earnings, M&A, IPOs)
  - 🔥 Sentiment (meme stocks, social)

### iOS Considerations:
- **PWA (Progressive Web App)** — Works in Safari, added to home screen
- No App Store needed
- No iOS security bypass required
- Works offline with cached data
- Simple tap to refresh

---

## 3. Technical Architecture

```
[data source]        [backend]           [frontend]
RSS feeds     →     Market Intel    →    PWA
Reddit             Pipeline              (HTML/JS)
                        ↓
                   news.json
                   (updated hourly)
```

### Backend:
- Market Intel cron already runs hourly
- Output: `data/news_feed.json`
- Format: `{category, title, source, url, timestamp, pattern}`

### Frontend:
- Single HTML file with JavaScript
- Fetches `news_feed.json`
- Displays in card format
- Filter buttons for categories
- Pull-to-refresh
