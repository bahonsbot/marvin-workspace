# ATS Review Checklist

Use this checklist for resume review, scoring, and tailoring passes.

## 1. Structure and readability

Check:
- standard section headings
- simple layout
- no gimmicky section names unless clearly readable
- no obvious table/graphic dependence if the format could be machine-read poorly
- consistent dates, titles, and bullet style

## 2. Role alignment

Check:
- target role is obvious quickly
- summary matches the real job target
- strongest relevant experience appears early
- skills reflect the actual target role

## 3. Keyword coverage

Check:
- must-have terms from the JD appear naturally where truthful
- important tools / functions / domains are present if supported by evidence
- no keyword stuffing

## 4. Evidence quality

Check:
- bullets describe achievements, not just duties
- outcomes are clear
- metrics are used where available
- leadership / ownership claims are appropriately sized

## 5. Risk flags

Flag:
- unsupported claims
- vague expertise language
- inflated seniority framing
- chronology confusion
- role mismatch
- empty summary language

## 6. Scoring posture

When giving a score, explain it briefly.
A simple frame is enough:
- strong fit
- decent fit with gaps
- weak fit as written

Do not turn the score into fake precision. The explanation matters more than the number.

## Output suggestion

For review passes, return:
1. overall read
2. top strengths
3. top weaknesses
4. ATS / keyword issues
5. highest-value fixes
6. optional rewrite examples
